What is the Track Trainer?

  • Stroke recovery as simple as playing a game.
  • No more endless repetition of mind-numbing tasks.
    No more frustration with confusing techniques.
    And no more breaking the bank.
  • The Track Trainer packages the effective Task Specific Training methodology into a game-like device that keeps rehabilitation motivational and exciting.

Why it Works


By transforming rehabilitation into a stimulating and customizable game, we’ve brought recovery within reach for all patients.

Simple

The Track Trainer is a fun, game-like rehabilitation device for hands, arms and shoulders.

Effective

Easily track improvement of patients over the course of therapy by recording lap times.

Affordable

Our simple design makes the Track Trainer one of the most affordable rehab devices in the market.
