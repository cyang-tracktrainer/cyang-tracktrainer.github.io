What is the Track Trainer?

At its core, the Track Trainer is comprised of a track through which a patient guides a joystick-like handle unit.

Several gates serve as obstacles by blocking the track at different points, and to open them, the patient must complete small tasks that incorporate the effective and scientifically-backed Task Specific Training methodology.

Finally, the device contains a timer that times each loop around the track, providing motivation to the patient and quantifiable metrics to the therapist.
