Our Core Values

Accountability

We pledge to meet our patients' demands for product refinement and customer service as best as we can.

Accessibility

We will listen to concerns of those who use our product and stay accessible to a wide range of patients.

Empathy

We are driven by the understanding that physical therapy is laborious/tedious for patients.

Adaptability

We will continue to research novel ways to improve our device.
