What is the Track Trainer?

Stroke recovery as simple as playing a game.

One of the most effective methods of physical rehabilitation is “Task Specific Training (TST),” which involves repetition of tasks that mimic regular daily actions.

The Track Trainer is a gamified device for recovering stroke patients that makes rehabilitation fun. Users guide the handle through a track, completing 6ST challenges along the way, and race to the finish line as fast as they can!

How It Works

Reach a gate

Complete the lit-up task

Repeat for 4 more gates

Beat your old time!
